# PrimeInt

> Nail your next interview.

AI-powered mock interviews that feel real. Role-specific questions. Feedback that actually helps you improve.

## What it does

- **Live mock interviews** — Voice-based interviews tailored to the exact role and company you are applying for.
- **Detailed scoring** — Clarity, structure, depth. See exactly where to tighten your answers.
- **Behavioral and technical** — From STAR-format stories to system design walkthroughs, all in one place.

## Why

Career. Part of a 20-product exploration of high-demand consumer and SMB markets.
